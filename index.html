Stack Snapshot

Questions up & down, start with where the idea came from and then work up and down the stack

What do you know?
How do you know?
WORLD IMPACT
What is the global impact?
When you exit, what lasting impact have you made?
EXIT
What is the end goal of the company?
Will you go public, be acquired, license, etc.?
SECTOR MAPPING
Describe the relationship between players in the industry 2 degrees from where you expect to be?
How will they respond?
COMPETITIVE MARKET
Who are the competitors that
sell the same/similar products? What do the users do now?
MARKET EXPANSION
What are follow-on or complementary products your company can offer? Long term what products and services will you offer?
COMPANY
What size and type of company would
be needed to support the business model?
BUSINESS MODEL
How will this make money?
How will customers pay for it?
CUSTOMER SEGMENT
Define and describe the target customers.
Who are you selling to? Describe the smallest cohesive group that is in the greatest need for your product. List 3 different groups of potential customers
SOLUTION
How is the product solving the problem?
What will the benefit to the user be?
PROBLEM
What problem is the pain causing?
PAIN SCALE
On a scale from death to mild inconvenience, how
would you categorize the impact of the pain?
PRODUCT
What is it? What are you selling?
REQUIREMENTS
What are the design constraints and requirements?
What does a successful product need to be or do?
DESIGN
What is the product description
that falls within the requirements?
INTEGRATIONS
What components/parts are in the design solution?
What do they do?
PRODUCTION
What platforms/systems do the components use?
How do you make the components work?
